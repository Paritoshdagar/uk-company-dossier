# Commercial licensing

This project is shared to help the community learn, experiment, and build practical understanding of Companies House data workflows. Non-commercial learning, research, teaching, prototyping, and community use are encouraged under the public licence.

If you would like to use the software in a commercial context, please seek an explicit commercial licence before you begin. That includes use in a business, paid client work, internal for-profit workflows, paid products or services, hosted services, paid support, or commercial consultancy.

The maintainer is open to thoughtful commercial conversations where the use case is clear and respectful of the community purpose of the project. To start one, open a [commercial licensing inquiry](https://github.com/Paritoshdagar/uk-company-dossier/issues/new?template=commercial-licensing.yml) with a high-level, non-confidential summary.

This page is a plain-language summary, not a substitute for the licence. The legally controlling text is [LICENSE](LICENSE). No commercial-use rights are granted unless and until a separate written licence has been agreed and signed.
