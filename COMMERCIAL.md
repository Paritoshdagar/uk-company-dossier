# Commercial licensing

The public licence does not grant commercial-use rights. Commercial use requires a separate written licence from the copyright holder.

Examples include use in paid client work, internal use by a for-profit organisation, incorporation into a paid product or service, and paid hosting, support, or consultancy based on the software.

This summary is not a substitute for the licence. To make an initial, non-confidential enquiry, open a [commercial licensing inquiry](../../issues/new?template=commercial-licensing.yml). The issue is public, and opening or submitting it does not grant permission or commercial-use rights; a separate written licence must be signed before any commercial use.
