# Contributing

Thank you for your interest. All external repository contributions are frozen until a UK intellectual-property solicitor has reviewed and approved the contributor agreement required for dual licensing. This freeze includes code, scripts, configuration, documentation, examples, patches, and other copyrightable or authored material.

You may open an issue to report a reproducible bug or propose a use case. While the freeze applies, reports must not include patches or substantial authored replacement content. Do not include credentials, private company information, or sensitive personal data.
